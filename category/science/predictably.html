Predictably Irrational by Dan Ariely

Predictably Irrational: The Hidden Forces That Shape Our Decisions is a 2008 book
by Dan Ariely, in which he challenges readers' assumptions about making decisions
based on rational thought. Ariely explains, "My goal, by the end of this book, is to
help you fundamentally rethink what makes you and the people around you tick. I hope
to lead you there by presenting a wide range of scientific experiments, findings,
and anecdotes that are in many cases quite amusing. Once you see how systematic
certain mistakes are�how we repeat them again and again�I think you will begin to learn how

to avoid some of them". The book has been republished in a "revised & expanded edition".
