Nudge

by Richard H. Thaler

Nudge is about choices�how we make them and how we can make better ones.
Drawing on decades of research in the fields of behavioral science and economics,
authors Richard H. Thaler and Cass R.
Sunstein offer a new perspective on preventing the countless mistakes we make� including ill-advised personal investments,
consumption of unhealthy foods, neglect of our natural resources, and other bad decisions.
